Our Story
"Our shop is meant to capture something that most people feel but can't quite name."

Erä Outfitters is coming to Historic Downtown Roswell — a meticulously curated field shop and gathering place.

We believe the best outdoor experiences start with being comfortable. Hobbies should be fun, not intimidating or pretentious.

We are here to break down barriers and make the things we love accessible to everyone. We are here to help you get started, improve your gear, or simply understand why we love what we love.

We hope to build a community centered around the ethos of the Finnish word erä.

Erä meaning place.

Place can be physical, like a stream or a field, or something intangible. Our shop is meant to capture something that most people feel but can’t quite name. We want to celebrate and share your passion for the places you love — where better than Roswell, Georgia.
