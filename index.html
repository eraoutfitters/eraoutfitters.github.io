Our Story
“Hobbies should be fun, not intimidating or pretentious.”

We are on a mission to build a community and Field & Fly Shop centered around the ethos of the Finnish word erä.

Erä (n.) Finnish — is an ancient word that holds a cultural meaning relating to the wilderness, hunting, fishing or a “share” of wild places. The concept of everyman’s freedom to roam in present-day Finland can be traced back to the ancient culture of erä.

We want to get to know you, equip you with our favorite gear and celebrate your passion for the wild places you love.

“The best days are the ones spent knee-deep in a river or walking a bird dog through open fields.”

— The Erä Ethos
